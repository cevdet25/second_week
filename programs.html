GROUP LESSONS

MOTIVATION AND ENERGY

Alternatives that appeal to different interests are waiting for you, where you can join free of charge, accompanied by professional DUMBBELL trainers.

CORE

Core training, which consists of the most basic exercises of a proper and strong posture, becomes much more efficient with the help of the trainer and the synergy created by the group. You can benefit from various core group lessons for different levels.

CYCLING

Cycling group lessons, which are performed with music and rhythm on the bike, and whose difficulty level is determined by using the rpm and level settings, bring together fun and sports. A wide variety of cycling classes that you can attend according to your level are waiting for you in our clubs.

CARDIO

If you want to get your heart rhythm accustomed to a high tempo and do good for your cardiovascular health, cardio group classes are just for you. You can benefit from cardio group classes that will help you lose weight and become more resistant with your intense tempo.

DANCE

The most fun way to stay fit is through group dance classes. Fun lessons from Zumba to Street Dance, from Hip Hop to Latin dance are waiting for you. You can take advantage of the dance lessons that are most suitable for you.
